1. What is a Program? (Short Paragraph)

A program is a set of instructions given to a computer to do a specific task. We use programs every day without noticing. For example, when I order food using Zomato, the app shows restaurants, takes my order, and tracks delivery because it follows a program made by developers.

4. Algorithm for Ordering Food on Swiggy

1. Open the Swiggy app.
2. Log in to your account.
3. Search for a restaurant.
4. Select the food items.
5. Add items to the cart.
6. Check the total amount.
7. Select the delivery address.
8. Choose a payment method.
9. Complete the payment.
10. Order is confirmed.
